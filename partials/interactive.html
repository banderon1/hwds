Cherriots Rideshare Web Site

Client: Cherriots Rideshare, Salem, OR

HWDS was commissioned to design an interactive Web site, a new logo identity and a marketing package to promote ridesharing and commute alternatives for the Salem-Keizer Transit District in Salem, Oregon.
