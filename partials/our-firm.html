Our Philosophy

Successful design requires the combination of three elements. The first is consistency. The second is creative imagination and conceptual development. The third is responsive service and follow-up. We integrate all three to give impeccable results, unmatched quality and superior customer service to provide you with an innovative campaign or marketing effort that produces results.

Our Staff's Qualifications

Harlan West, President and Creative Director

For the past 25 years, Mr. West has been art-directing, designing and illustrating hundreds of publications and Web sites for government agencies and elected officials, public-transportation agencies, healthcare firms, and financial institutions. His award-winning designs and illustrations have been featured in annual reports, magazines and catalogs, as well as on billboards. His multimedia work includes the design of Web sites, interactive CD-ROMs, e-newsletters, animations and kiosks. Previous to becoming full-time creative director at HWDS and Associates, Inc., Mr. West served as the art director/graphics manager for the Southern California Association of Governments (SCAG) and Commuter Transportation Services (CTS) for more than thirteen years. Here, he managed an in-house creative department and oversaw the development of hundreds of publications relating to public transit, regional planning, housing, and solid waste disposal. In 2003, he became the full-time Creative Director for our firm. Here, he has developed and managed projects for Norwalk Transit, Metrolink, Metro, MWCOG, Time Warner Cable and Kaiser Permanente, to name a few. He regularly advises public relations directors and senior management on the best solutions for achieving their communications and marketing strategic goals. He holds a bachelor of fine arts in design and illustration from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and a bachelor of arts in political science/public administration from the University of California at Santa Barbara. More recently, he has completed additional post-graduate coursework in digital animation, Web design, Flash and marketing principles at UCLA Extension and continually updates his skills by attending classes and seminars. Moreover, he is highly adept at project and print management, project budgeting, vendor coordination and client presentations.

Jessie McCann, Assistant Art Director

Ms. McCann is highly skilled in the design of new marketing materials and corporate identity. She is a true expert in print production and overseeing print quality and the integrity of design approach. She helps design monthly newsletters for Kaiser Permanente, and has contributed to the design of numerous event promotions as well as the design of various materials for Metrolink and Metro. Ms. McCann has a keen eye and is adept at compositing photographic imagery and is highly skilled in the creation of Web sites, Web pages, e-newsletters and e-blasts. Ms. Mcann has been a major part of the HWDS team since 2009.

Peter Williamson, Graphic Designer and Production Artist

Mr. Williamson assists in the execution of design elements, layout of text, design of logos and Web icons and creation of Photoshop collages. He helps with the design, layout and production of graphic elements and the execution of campaign graphics and advertisements, bus wraps, promotional materials, collaterals and newsletters. He has worked for HWDS for nearly nine years and has assisted with the design of many publications for governmental clients such as Metro, Norwalk Transit and the City of Santa Monica.

Mrs. West has been instrumental in recruiting new talent for our firm and helping to manage the office. She interviews new employees and reviews portfolios. She often develops catchy headlines and slogans for client newspaper advertisements and promotions.
