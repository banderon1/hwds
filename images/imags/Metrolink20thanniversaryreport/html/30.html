CHAPTER
PTC Milestones
September 2008
SCRRA Board of Directors directs the Chief Executive Officer to pursue
funding for safety enhancements from the "North American Joint Positive
Train Control Program" funded by the Federal Railroad Administration and
any other sources of federal or state funding eligible for developing,
implementing or operating a Positive Train Control system for Metrolink.
October 2008
The Rail Safety and Improvement Act of 2008 is signed into law,
requiring installation of Positive Train Control systems.
February 2009
CRRA establishes organization to proactively develop and deliver PTC by
December 2012.
January 2010

The Federal Railroad Administration issues its final rule requiring railroads
to install Positive Train Control technology.
March 2010

SCRRA issues Request for Proposal (RFP) for the
Vendor/Integrator component of the project.
April 2010

SCRRA submits PTC Implementation Plan (PTCIP) to the
Federal Railroad Administration.
May 2010

Federal Railroad Administration conditionally approves SCRRA PTC
Implementation Plan.
October 2010

Award Vendor/Integrator contract to Parsons Transportation Group.
February 2011

PTC Development Plan (PTCDP) Variance Type Approval
submitted to FRA.
July 2011
Onboard pilot installations begin.
August 2011
Draft PTC Safety Plan submitted to the FRA for informal review.
October 2011
Award communications backhaul to Parsons Environmental
and Infrastructure.
February 2012
ETMS VII brake testing conducted on the BNSF San Bernardino
Subdivision.
June 2012
Interoperability communications testing with UP and BNSF.
